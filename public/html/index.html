About Us

Quality and integrity are the soul of Red Leaf Bistro. We have great passion and pride for what serve from our kitchen. Everytime someone dines with us, we see it as a way to share our story through our food. We only strive to serve the highest quality foods. Our family oringally started in the catering business but as time passed we releazied we could more than before. We look forward to your new memories here.
